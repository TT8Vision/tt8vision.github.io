The Person Behind The Brand

MEET
NOAH
KERCHHOFF.

"All people were made with a uniqueness true to them — and that's exactly why every brand we manufacture for deserves garments as individual as the people wearing them."

Noah grew up on the Cape Flats — sketching clothes for figurines before he could afford fabric. That obsession never left. IMADETHEARK started as his own label, built from scratch with his own hands, and the brand's credibility opened doors: schools, clothing startups, corporates, and collectives started coming to him not just to buy — but to build.

When you book a call with IMADETHEARK, you're talking to someone who has been exactly where you are. Noah understands the risk of your first order, the pressure of a school deadline, the vision behind a brand that doesn't exist yet. That's not a pitch — it's just the truth.
